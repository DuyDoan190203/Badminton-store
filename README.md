# RacketRain
Badminton-Ecommerce developed by NET , REACT and Redux. 
# User Instructions
## Here's how to get started with the RacketRain Badminton E-commerce App:

### Browsing Products
Use the search bar to find specific items or browse categories to explore our range of products.
Filter and sort results to find products that meet your preferences in terms of price, brand, and specifications.
Tap on a product to view detailed information, including descriptions, specifications, reviews, and pricing.
### Purchasing Products
Once you've selected a product, choose your preferred size or color (if applicable) and tap 'Add to Cart'.
Review your cart to ensure the details are correct, and then proceed to checkout.
Enter your shipping information, choose your payment method, and confirm your order.
### Managing Your Account
Login and Access your account 
View your order history by clicking to the cart
